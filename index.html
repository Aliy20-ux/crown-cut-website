About Crown Cut

Situated in the heart of Gilmore Place, Crown Cut is built on the belief that a great haircut is one of life's non-negotiable pleasures. We blend traditional barbering technique with contemporary style — because you deserve both.

Every client who walks through our door receives the same level of attention. We take time to understand what you want, advise on what'll suit you, and deliver a result you'll notice every time you look in the mirror.
